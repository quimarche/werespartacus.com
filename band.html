We’re Spartacus are a 4.5 piece band performing an eclectic cocktail of pop and rock, hits and oddities including tracks by David Bowie, Led Zeppelin, The Police, Elvis Costello, The Kinks, Terravision, Red Hot Chilli Peppers, Hendrix, Madness and etc…. etc…. and can mainly seen performing at pubs, clubs, parties and beer festivals around the north west.
